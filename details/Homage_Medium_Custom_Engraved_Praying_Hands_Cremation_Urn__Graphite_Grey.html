Homage Medium Cremation Urn

This elegant cremation urn is designed to honor your loved one with grace and significance. Its praying hands theme represents peace, love, and comfort during the grieving process, making it more than just a container—it's a symbol of enduring remembrance.

Crafted in a sophisticated graphite grey finish, the urn blends seamlessly with any home decor, offering a timeless and serene presence. Whether placed in a living room, study, or office, it serves as a quiet tribute to the person who once filled those spaces with warmth.

The secure threaded lid ensures that the ashes are safely kept, providing a sense of lasting closure. The addition of a soft felt bottom protects furniture and surfaces, so the urn can be displayed without causing damage.

With a capacity of 80 cubic inches, this urn is perfectly suited to hold the ashes of a person weighing up to 90 lbs. It's an ideal choice for those looking for a personalized and meaningful way to celebrate a life well-lived.

Custom engraving adds a unique message to the design, allowing you to make it even more special. This urn is more than a memorial—it's a lasting piece of art that honors your loved one in style.
