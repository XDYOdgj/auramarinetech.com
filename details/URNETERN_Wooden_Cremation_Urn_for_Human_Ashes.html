URNETERN Handcrafted Wooden Cremation Urn - A Timeless Tribute

The natural pine wood showcases a unique grain pattern, highlighting the individuality of every piece and making it an elegant memorial for your loved one. The bottom opening is securely fastened with four stainless steel screws, ensuring a safe and reliable storage solution for ashes or pet remains. This design not only guarantees durability but also provides peace of mind for families seeking a dignified way to honor their departed.
